About Us

God Help Those Who Help Themselves

Assemblies Of God Fellowship is open to worshippers of all ages and backgrounds. We lead our congregation in worship, deepening the connection to God, understanding the teachings of Christ, and ultimately to a vibrant faith. Through the dedication of our leadership, members and volunteers, we are committed to the teachings of Jesus Christ, and are here to spread His message.

In this sacred space, we gather as a united congregation, intertwining our voices in heartfelt worship, as we strive to deepen our connection with the Divine. Here, the teachings of Christ are brought to life, illuminating our minds and hearts, and leading us towards a faith that thrives with vibrancy and Within our fellowship, you will find a family of dedicated souls who share a profound commitment to the teachings of Jesus Christ. Our leadership, members, and volunteers pour their hearts and souls into creating an environment where His message can truly flourish. Guided by unwavering devotion, they tirelessly strive to spread the transformative power of His love, kindness, and wisdom to all who seek solace and spiritual growth.

As you step into our community, you will be enveloped by a sense of belonging, love, and acceptance. We believe that every individual is a precious thread in the tapestry of our fellowship, each one contributing a unique perspective and story that enriches our collective journey.

Our Vision

Assemblies Of God Fellowship is an inclusive church committed to Christ and His word. We are dedicated to Restoring the ancient Landmarks, Lifting up a standard and raising kingdom builders. Our church is not just a house of worship. At its core are the members and believers that come together to practice God’s word, celebrate His love and extend His gifts to all.

Our Mission

The mission of Assemblies Of God Fellowship Church is to faithfully carry ouft God's calling by spreading Christ's love, upholding the teachings of the Bible, restoring ancient landmarks, setting a high moral standard, empowering kingdom builders, welcoming all who seek God, and celebrating the gifts and blessings God bestows upon us.
